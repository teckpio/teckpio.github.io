How Pay Rate is extracted:

There are 3 method to determine Pay Rate to be applied to Pay Quantity

1. a value ($)

2. a % to be applied to a processed PayItem or PayItem

3. a % / $ extracted from a matched range (of processed PayItem or PayItem value OR quantity of a PayUnit).

* If % is used, the % is to be applied to a processed PayItem or PayItem
