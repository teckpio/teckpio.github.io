Functional Description

Dr How is a mobile/web application that enables patients to manage their appointments with registered doctors.

Doctors who wish to register with Dr How system also use Dr How mobile/web application for registration.

Menu Options

1 Home Back to Homepage
2 Dashboard Today's appointments
3 Announcement Review Doctor's announcements
4 New Appointment To make new appointment
5 Profile Patient's profile
6 Appointments Appointment Records

Process Flow of Appointments

Select Doctor -> Select Consultation Mode -> Select Date -> Select Priority -> Make Payment -> Register

Making New Appointment

1 Search Doctor There are 3 ways to search for doctor:

1 SEARCH Search doctors that match all or any of the following criteria:

1. Doctor's Name
2. Clinic's name
3. Patient's Symptoms
3. Doctor's Specialty
2 FIND NEARBY Search for doctors/clinics which are within a preset distance from Patient's current location.
3 Text Pattern Search for text entered in the <Text Search> in the following fields:

1. Doctor's name
2. Clinic's name
3. Doctor's qualification
4. Distance(Km)

On the doctor's row in the extracted list:

1 Detail To show details of selected doctor including Symptoms, Specialty and Pricing.
2 Select Select doctor for appointment.

2 Choose Consultation Mode There are 4 modes to choose from:

1. Attend
2. Tele-Conference
3. Tele-Video
4. Text-Messaging

For the selected mode:

1 DETAIL To show business hours and rates of consultation fees for selected mode.
2 SELECT Select mode for appointment.

3 Choose Date

Select required date of the appointment.

Number of registered appointments for the selected date is indicated by Color Codes.

4 Choose Priority

Patients can choose 2 priorities for the appointment:

1. Elective
2. Premier

A slot and estimated time will be assigned to an Elective appointment only when the appointment is registered after the appointment is made. Elective appointments will be arranged to be after all Priority appointments.

A slot will be assigned to a Premier appointment after the appointment is made and before its registration. During calculation of slot sequnce and appointments' estiamted times, an unregistered Premier appointment will be taken into account although it's not being registered yet. Therefore, when a Premier appointment is being registered, it will be slotted ahead of Elective appontments when determining estimated time.

Patient keys in any additional remark for doctor in the <Patient Remark> box.

For Tele-Conference appointment, Patient is required to key in the phone number that Doctor will call in <Phone Number> box.

After accepting the Terms and Conditions, Patient will proceed to make payment.

Making Payment

Dr How is using IPay88 for online payment method.

Review Appointments

Review appointments made by clicking the <Appointments> menu item. Appointments made are categorised into 3 tabs:

1. Today
2. Future
3. History

Click <RETRIEVE> of the selected tab to retrieve appointment records.

Registering Appointment

Appointments made can be registered in 2 ways:

1. Dashboard: for today's appointments only.
2. Appointments: select appointments from retrieved list for registration.

Unpaid appointments will not be able to register.

Unpaid appointment could happen if for some technical reasons (eg system hangs while patients are making payment in IPay88), the appoinment do not finish the payment process. In this case, the appointment will have an Unpaid Status in the list. Clicking RETRIEVE of the selected tab will clear appointments made without payment.

Registred appointments will have an Estiamted Time assigned. Unregistered appointments will show "Yet to register".

Unregistered appointments will not appear in Doctor's appointment list for consultations.

Day of Appointment and Estimated Time

On the day of the appointment, it will appear in the Dashboard with an Estimated Time based on Doctor/Clinic's business hours when Patient registered the appointment.

Estimated Time of the appointment will be updated as Doctor conducts his/her consultation sessions during the day. To retrieve an appointment's latest estimated time, Patient need to click REFRESH.

Estimated Time Remark
HOLD Doctor is holding the consultation session.
TO BE SERVED When the Patient is next to be served.

If Doctor has overrun his previous consultation session, the Estimated Time shown before might not be accurate.
Current Time Doctor has overrun previous appointments and the appointment is not the next to be served.
DONE Appointment's consultation session has ended.

At Time of Appointment

1 Attend Attend to the clinic.
2 Tele-Conference Patient needs to be ready by the phone of the registered number in the appointment to wait for Doctor's call.
3 Tele-Video Patients needs to go into the Video Call page and wait for Doctor to create a video chat room. Patient will be prompted when Doctor has created the video chat room, after which Patient will join into the video chat room by pressing CONNECT.
4 Text-Messaging Patients to check with text messaging for Doctor's reply.
